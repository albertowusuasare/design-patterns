- The decorator design pattern is used to add behavoir to existing objects at runtime.
- The decorator pattern enforces the open closed principle.
- The open-closed principle says that classes should be open for extension but closed for modification.
- The decorator pattern is ideal for situations where there is a likelihood of an explosion of subtypes.
- In this example, we shall model a starbucks menu system. Here, we shall have basic starbucks beverages as well as
several condiments.
- The final description and cost of the drink will be a function of the base beverage plus the various condiments that
the drink is composed of.
